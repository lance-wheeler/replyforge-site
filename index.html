How it works

Four phases. Forty-five days to predictable pipeline.

We don't blast volume. We build infrastructure. The first 14 days are setup. Weeks 3–6 we ramp. From month 2 onward, your calendar fills with qualified calls.

01
Days 1–3
Discovery & ICP

60-min kickoff call. We extract your ideal customer profile, value prop, objection patterns, and target outcomes. You get a written ICP brief and campaign strategy doc within 48 hours of signing.

02
Days 3–10
Infrastructure

We provision 3–10 sending domains and 12–40 inboxes. SPF, DKIM, DMARC configured. Native warmup runs for 7–10 days. Your primary brand domain is never used for cold sends — it stays clean.

03
Days 10–14
Copy & Targeting

We write your 5–7 step sequence using proven outbound frameworks, AI-assisted personalization, and manual review. Lists are pulled from Apollo and verified for accuracy. You approve everything before we send a single email.

04
Day 14+
Launch & Optimize

Campaigns go live. Daily volume ramps from 20 to 40 emails per inbox. Replies are auto-categorized by AI and routed to your calendar. Weekly reports. Bi-weekly A/B tests. You see meetings on your calendar by week 4.

For B2B in most jurisdictions, yes — when done correctly. CAN-SPAM (US) permits opt-out-based commercial email with required sender identity, physical address, and unsubscribe handling. GDPR (EU) permits B2B outreach under a documented legitimate interest assessment with relevance and easy opt-out. CASL (Canada) is stricter; we exclude Canadian addresses by default unless you specifically request and document implied consent. Every email we send includes a compliance-aware footer with sender identity, physical address, and one-click opt-out. We process unsubscribes within 24 hours, well inside the 10-business-day legal requirement under CAN-SPAM. We're compliance-aware operators — you should still get your own legal review before any major campaign.
